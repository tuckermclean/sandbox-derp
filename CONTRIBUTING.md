# Contributing

Thanks for wanting to contribute to `sandbox-derp`, the world's leading platform for
doing nothing at scale. Adding to a repository of nothing is a delicate art, but the
process is mercifully simple.

## How to Propose a Change

1. **Fork the repo.** Click the fork button and make `sandbox-derp` your own. It will
   contain exactly as much as the original, which is to say, the same amount.
2. **Create a branch.** Give it a short, descriptive name (`fix-typo`, `add-poem`,
   `improve-nothing`). Working on `main` directly is discouraged.
3. **Make your change.** Keep it focused. One idea per branch makes for a happier
   reviewer.
4. **Open a pull request.** Describe what you changed and why. If it closes an issue,
   mention it (e.g. `Closes #N`).

## Be Nice

Be kind, be respectful, and assume good faith. Reviews are about the change, not the
person. We are all here to make nothing a little bit better together.

That's it. Welcome aboard, and thanks for contributing.
